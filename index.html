NEOTALA

Our pursuit.

Progress against

Breast cancer.

NCT02684032 Listen to this page Email Print

join the fight against triple negative breast cancer.

NEOTALA is a clinical research study assessing weather a study drug is safe and effective when given as a potential treatment for triple negative breast cancer.

To see if you may qualify for this clinical trial, please click here or call 1-800-254-6398.

Who may participate

Along with the eligibility criteria listed here, NEOTALA study could be an option for you if you are willing to provide blood and tumor tissue sample for genetic testing, and if you have not received any antitumor treatment for this cancer.

Condition

Triple Negative Breast Cancer

Sex

What to expect

This is a single arm-study, meaning that all participants will receive the study drug.you can expect to take the study drug by mouth once a day for about 24 weeks.After this we will schedule brest surgery for you within 4 to 6 weeks.

During the study, you can expect to visit the clinical for health assessments,such as medical questioneries,physical examinations, blood and urine samples and body scans.This helps us to contunially monitor how your brest cancer is responding.

We will continue to contact you regularly by telephone for upto 5 years to see how you are.The time can expect to be in the study really depends upon a few things,including how well you will tolerate the study drug and how your cancer responds.
